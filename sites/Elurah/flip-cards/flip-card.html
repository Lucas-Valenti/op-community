Dwarves



Dwarves are a proud and resilient race of subterranean creatures, renowned for their unparalleled skill as miners and craftsmen.
Elves



Elves are a magical people of otherworldly grace, living in the world but not entirely part of it.
Gnomes



Gnomes are a diminutive race of skilled craftsmen and curious adventurers.
Halflings



Halflings are small and nimble humanoids that often go unnoticed by larger races, known for their exceptional stealth and dexterity.
Humans



The youngest of the common races, Humans strive to achieve as much as they can in the short years they are given.
Orcs



Orcs live for conflict. Peace is the property of a perfect world. In a world that is imperfect, a lack of conflict is a failure to fight.
